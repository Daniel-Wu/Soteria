Safe, High Quality, Low Cost Healthcare.

Soteria devices empower doctors with low-cost devices to examine all patients, regardless of geograhical location, at any time with smooth, fast, AI-driven video feeds. But Soteria takes it one step further. Soteria protects refugee privacy and immigration status by allowing them to blur their faces on the video feed. We at Soteria believe that all people should have access to high quality healthcare without strings attached.

Our Goal

To create a high-quality, accessible, platform for doctors and patients to communicate safely and effectively.
